Lock Card

Team: Me, 1 Visual Designer,1 Creative Director, Strategist who scheduled interviews
Role: UX Designer
Timeline: 1 week
Scale: Thousands of users
Technology: Figma

Problem: If a card is lost or stolen, users need a method of locking their card. This was part of a larger batch of features. Timeline to delivery was one week - 2 days for designing, 2 days for testing and synthesis, 1 day for presentation and tweaks.

Process:

  • Created an experience canvas for the lock/unlock card process including storyboarded scenarios and research questions
  • designed happy path workflow and reviewed against earlier competitive analysis
  • Wrote a simple research script and built two prototypes - one single card and one multicard
  • Tested the design with 8 people from our target user groups.

Outcome: All users easily understood the feature and reacted positively. The clients created scope for the backlog to add the "recurring charges" feature. A more robust authorized users workflow were added and integrated throughout the app.
